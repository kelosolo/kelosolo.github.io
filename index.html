My Battle 3 Wheeled Robot Car

This 3 wheeled robot car use lego Mindstorm EV3 brick to power it.I built a base to hold the two moters and the brick.Then I added some armour to it because we are having battle to see who can wrestle our cars.We controled our cars by using the Lego Commander app on our phones and we used bluetooth to connect the EV3 brick on our robot.Then we made a joystick on our commander app and then we started playing with our robots.

EV3D4 Robot

This robot also uses Lego Mindstorm EV3 brick.The EV3D4 is like the Star Wars robot R2D2.it looks like it, and it walks like R2D2 too!It uses the Infarned Sensor,touch sensors,colour sensors,3 motors etc.I used the Lego Mindstorm programming software to programe it to move,make sounds and stuff.
